SUPERMARKET — SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
=================================

Thanks for buying Supermarket! Getting it onto your phone takes about
30 seconds — no coding, no hosting, no account to create.


STEP 1 — Open the app link
---------------------------
On your phone, open this address in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android):

    https://giorgiob-ux.github.io/supermarket-app/

Everything you add in the app stays on your own phone — nothing is
ever sent anywhere.


STEP 2 — Add it to your home screen
-------------------------------------
1. iPhone: tap the Share icon (square with an arrow up) → "Add to
   Home Screen" → Add.
   Android: tap the browser's menu (⋮) → "Install app" or "Add to
   Home Screen".
2. Open Supermarket from your home screen, just like any other app.

From this point on it works completely offline — turn on airplane
mode and it still works, because everything is stored on your phone.


STEP 3 — Start using it
-------------------------
- Tap + to create your first list.
- Type grocery items in English or Italian — the app recognizes
  hundreds of common items automatically.
- Tap the ⇅ icon to sort by aisle, alphabetically, or by most
  recently added.
- Go to ⚙️ Settings to edit the aisle layout so it matches YOUR
  supermarket (it ships with an example layout — customizing it
  takes a couple of minutes and makes aisle-sorting far more useful).

See README.md in this folder for more detail on every feature, and
LICENSE.txt for the terms of use.


PREFER TO HOST YOUR OWN COPY?
-------------------------------
This zip also includes the full app source (index.html, style.css,
the icons/ and js/ folders). If you'd rather run your own copy instead
of using the link above, drag this whole folder onto
https://app.netlify.com/drop (free) and it'll give you your own
web address to use in Step 1 instead.


TROUBLESHOOTING
----------------
- "It won't install / no 'Add to Home Screen' option": make sure
  you're using Safari on iPhone or Chrome on Android (not an in-app
  browser like Instagram's).
- "I lost my lists": if you ever clear your phone's browser data or
  switch phones, you'll lose everything unless you had exported a
  backup first. Go to ⚙️ Settings → Backup → Export data regularly.
